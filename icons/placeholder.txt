# GlucoBalance PWA Icons

This directory contains the PWA icons for GlucoBalance.

## Required Icon Sizes:
- 32x32 (favicon)
- 72x72 (Android)
- 96x96 (Android)
- 128x128 (Android)
- 144x144 (Android)
- 152x152 (iOS)
- 180x180 (iOS)
- 192x192 (Android, maskable)
- 384x384 (Android)
- 512x512 (Android, maskable)

## Icon Design Guidelines:
- Use Azure Blue (#007FFF) as primary color
- White background for standard icons
- Ensure maskable icons have safe area padding
- Include medical/health symbolism (heart, plus sign, etc.)
- Maintain brand consistency across all sizes

## Maskable Icons:
Icons marked as "maskable" should have important content within the safe area (center 80% of the icon) to ensure they display correctly when masked by the system.
